History of the Kona, Hawaii Temple!

The Kona, Hawaii Temple was the second temple built in Hawaii and was announced on May 7, 1998. The ceremony was presided over by John B. Dickson of the Seventy. The Temple was dedicated by President Gordon B. Hinckley on January 23rd - 24th, 2000.
