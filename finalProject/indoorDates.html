Binge-Watch A Show/Movie!

This one is pretty self explainatory, find a show and watch it!

It could be a show neither of you have seen but are interested in!

OR

A show you have both watched before and liked!

You can never go wrong with a good Disney movie!

--Snacks optional, but definitely recommended--

At Home Game Night

Video games or board games? Either! Just make sure it's something you can both have fun with!

Other people make this more fun, so bring along another couple or two!

Bring out yours' and your date's competitive sides!

--Snacks optional, but definitely recommended--

Indoor Entertainment in the City

There are a variety of options for having some fun in the town or city that you're in!

  • Arcade
  • Bowling
  • Movie Theatre
  • Museum
  • Restaurant
  • Pet Store
  • Book Store
  • Escape Room
  • Mini Golf

Should be noted that most, if not all, of these will cost some money. You should see how much it'll cost you before you go.

Some places offer a discount on certain days for certain activities if you have a date, so look into that too!

Cooking/Baking

There are a variety of options for this activity!

  • Bake desserts
  • Learn a new recipe together
  • Go to the grocery store and find random things and make food out of it and then eat it!
  • Make your meal for the day/night
  • Make your own ice cream

Make sure your date doesn't have any allergies! You don't want to kill them or make them sick!

Come up with a budget and stick to it! Most likely, this date will cost money too!
